Our cordless vacuums and floor washers are certified for major export markets — so you can sell with confidence. Certificates and test reports are available on request.

Standards We Meet

Certifications by market

Applicable certifications vary by model and destination — confirmed per project during sampling.

CE

Europe (EEA) — conformity with EU health, safety and environmental protection standards.

FCC

United States — electromagnetic compatibility for electronic devices sold in the US market.

RoHS

EU — restriction of hazardous substances (lead, mercury, cadmium and more) in electronics.

UL1017

North America — UL safety standard for vacuum cleaners and motor-operated cleaning appliances.

UN38.3 / UN3481

Lithium battery transport safety — required for the safe air and sea shipping of battery packs.

More on request

Additional market certifications (e.g. UKCA, PSE, SAA) can be arranged on request.
