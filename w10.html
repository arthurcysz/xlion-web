Key Features

Vacuum and mop in a single pass

Wet & Dry 2-in-1

Vacuums debris and washes the floor at the same time — one pass, clean and dry.

180° Flat Recline

Reclines fully to 180° with a 60° swivel — glides under sofas, beds and low furniture.

Self-Cleaning & Dry

One-touch cycle deep-cleans the roller and tubing, then centrifugally air-dries it. Built-in pet-hair strainer.

Edge-to-Corner

Close-to-edge brush design cleans right up against walls and skirting.

Dual Water Tanks

800 ml clean + 450 ml dirty tanks always use fresh water; ≥85% water recovery.

Up to 50 min Runtime

Cordless freedom with enough endurance for whole-home wet cleaning.
