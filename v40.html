V40 Cordless Vacuum Cleaner

This lightweight cordless vacuum is built for pet hair with an anti-tangle brush roll that resists hair wrap. The 450W brushless motor delivers 105AW of powerful suction, while the 5-stage + HEPA filtration traps fine dust and allergens. It runs up to 60 minutes, and the LED headlight reveals hidden debris. The floor head tilts 180° up/down and stands 90° on its own. Empty the dustbin with one click — no mess. The smart display clearly shows mode, battery and alerts. Remove the brush roll instantly for cleaning. The telescopic aluminum wand adjusts length, and it is wall-mountable with built-in accessory storage. With 3 ways to charge, it is perfect for sofas, curtains, carpets, tiles and hard floors — an ideal choice for bedrooms, living rooms, kitchens and other indoor home settings.
