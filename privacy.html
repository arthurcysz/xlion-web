Privacy Policy

How Suzhou X-Lion Electric Co., Ltd collects, uses and protects your information.

Last updated: 6 July 2026

1. Who we are

This website (x-lion.com.cn) is operated by Suzhou X-Lion Electric Co., Ltd ("X-LION", "we", "us"). For any privacy question, contact us at contact@x-lion.com.cn.

2. Information we collect

  • Inquiry form: when you submit an enquiry, we collect the name, company, email, phone, country/region, product of interest and message you provide.
  • Analytics: with your consent, we collect anonymous usage data — pages viewed, device/browser type, approximate location and referral source — via cookies (Google Analytics 4).

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide quotes or product information.
  • To understand website traffic and improve our content and user experience.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Cookies & consent

We use analytics cookies only after you click "Accept" on our cookie banner. If you decline, no analytics cookies are set and the site still works fully. You can change your choice by clearing your browser storage for this site.

5. Third-party services

  • Google Analytics (Google LLC) — website analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Web3Forms — delivers your inquiry-form submissions to our email. Data is not stored beyond delivery.
  • Links to Amazon and Tergeo stores are external sites governed by their own privacy policies.

6. Data retention

Inquiry details are kept only as long as needed to handle your request and our business relationship. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics settings.

7. Your rights

Subject to applicable law (including the EU GDPR), you may request access to, correction or deletion of your personal data, object to processing, or withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, email contact@x-lion.com.cn.

8. International transfers

As we operate internationally, your data may be processed outside your country. We take reasonable steps to protect it in line with this policy.
