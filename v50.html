V50 Cordless Vacuum Cleaner

This cordless vacuum is engineered to make whole-home cleaning effortless and efficient. The specialized anti-tangle floor brush prevents hair wrap, making it the ideal choice for pet hair, while bright LED headlights and a 180°/90° swivel head help you spot dust in dark corners and glide easily under furniture. Powered by a strong motor and a 22.2V high-capacity battery, it delivers up to 45 minutes of fade-free runtime in standard mode (25 minutes in mid, 15 minutes in max) — perfect for deep cleaning without frequent recharging. Breathe easier with the advanced 5-layer fully sealed filtration system, which captures 99.99% of microscopic dust particles as small as 0.3 microns, ensuring cleaner air for your family, especially in homes with pets or children. The adjustable aluminum tube extends from 15.4 to 24 inches, allowing users of all heights to clean ceilings or floors comfortably, and the lightweight ergonomic handle reduces wrist strain during extended cleaning sessions. Plus, the unique 90° upright lock lets the vacuum stand on its own anywhere — no wall drilling required. The one-click dust bin emptying system and washable filter components make maintenance hassle-free while preserving long-term suction performance.
